Am kürzeren Ende der Sonnenallee
Thomas Brussig

This edition of Thomas Brussig's Am kürzeren Ende der Sonnenallee is designed to overlay personal and public accounts of key events during the period of 1945-1990 that are referenced in the novel. It links keywords in the text to digitized primary sources containing both public and private accounts of these events to complement fictional accounts from the novel. These sources are pre-loaded into Voyant for easy visualization and statistical anlysis. This range of fictional, non-fictional, public, and private perspectives helps convey a rich and multi-layered understanding of life in the GDR.

Editors: Sibel Sayılı-Hurley and Claudia Lynn
